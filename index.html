About Crumbs Bakery

Founded in 2023, Crumbs Bakery is an artisanal bakehouse dedicated to crafting exceptional pastries using time-honored techniques and premium ingredients. Our team of classically trained bakers combines European tradition with modern innovation to deliver unforgettable flavors.

Our Commitment

  • Quality Ingredients: We source organic flour, local dairy, and seasonal produce
  • Small-Batch Baking: Every item is handcrafted daily in our open kitchen
  • Sustainability: Zero food waste initiative through our "Day-Old Bread" donation program
